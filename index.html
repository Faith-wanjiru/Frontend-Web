Summary For My First Week At Akirachix

Warm Welcome

On 27th January, We were warmly welcomed at the school by the alumnis as they were showing us our respective rooms
and the parents were at the Kaya as they took their tea and waited for the next session which was an introductory part
of the scholarship program which was later followed by signing of the the legal contract.

Assigning of duties and clearly reading the school policies

The Director of the school clearly read out the school policies as she highlighted the values that should be upheld by the students during their stay
in the scholarship program.Also there was assigning of the various duties which we must do to ensure that cleaniness is maintained.

Good Things I Experienced in Akirachix
  1. Warm Welcome
  2. Well cooked food
  3. Introduction to various units
  4. Getting connected with my mentor
  5. Meeting our trainers and the various units they train on

Bad Things I Experienced In Akirachix
  • Limited time breaks during lessons

Conclusion

In overall, I am motivated and challenged to continue to pursue the program since the school motto is "She Builds, She Serves, She Leads"
and I believe that I can achieve my desired goals and dreams.
